Namkeen Broast🍗

Namkeen Broast is a lighter, savory variation of traditional fried chicken that highlights the natural flavors of the meat with minimal, non-spicy seasoning. Unlike heavily spiced zinger options, it relies primarily on salt, black pepper, garlic, and vinegar or lemon.

Multan Desi food

Multan Desi foodMultan features an incredibly rich and distinct food culture deeply rooted in the traditions of South Asia. From slow-cooked, rich gravies to succulent charcoal street barbecue and its globally famous crystalline sweets, the City of Saints serves some of the most authentic flavors in Pakistan.

Zia Family Food industries Private Limited

Zia Family Food Industries Private Limited (operating widely as Family Food Products or Zia Foods) is a prominent Pakistani food manufacturing and exporting company based in Multan, Punjab. Established originally in 1981 by Haji Abu Bakar, the company has grown from a local Southern Punjab enterprise into an international manufacturer specializing in halal-certified snacks, confectionery, and everyday pantry items.
